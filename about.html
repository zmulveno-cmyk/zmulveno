Zoei Mulvenon

I am a third-year Media Arts and Sciences major at Arizona State University, passionate about combining creativity and technology. I have experience in design, digital media, and web development. I enjoy creating visually engaging projects using HTML, CSS, animations, and storytelling. I am proficient with tools such as Adobe Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop, Canva, and Max 9. I continually explore new software to expand my creative toolkit. My goal is to build projects that are not only aesthetically striking but also user-friendly and innovative.

In addition to my technical work, I have a strong interest in philosophy and creative writing, which informs the way I approach storytelling, conceptual thinking, and problem-solving in design. I thrive in collaborative and fast-paced environments, bringing creativity, critical thinking, and attention to detail to every project. I aim to create innovative, user-centered work that combines art, technology, writing, and thoughtful design.
